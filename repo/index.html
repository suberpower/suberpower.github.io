Plantenbiebs Nederland

To make contribution to society by creating apps to stimulate plant exchange in the Benelux. Plantenbiebs Nederland is founded in the summer of 2020. In the year of lockdowns and social isolation, we have found ways in cheer and comfort in houseplants. We cut stems and grow “stekjes” to trade locally via “plantenbiebs”.
